# EDA Findings

1. NAV values generally increased from 2022 to 2026.
2. SBI Mutual Fund showed the highest AUM among major fund houses.
3. SIP inflows reached their peak in May 2025 in the dataset.
4. Equity funds attracted higher investments than debt funds.
5. The 26–35 age group contributed significantly to SIP investments.
6. Male investors formed a larger share of transactions.
7. SIP investment amounts varied across age groups.
8. Investor participation was observed across both T30 and B30 cities.
9. Some states contributed substantially higher SIP amounts than others.
10. Fund categories and risk profiles showed diverse distribution across schemes.
